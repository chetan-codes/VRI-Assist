VRI Assist

VR-enabled Disabilities Detection

GET STARTED

VRI Assist is a VR-enabled disabilities detection app that helps people with disabilities to get the support they need. With VRI Assist, you can easily connect with a qualified disability specialist to get help with tasks such as:

  • Finding and applying for government benefits
  • Navigating the healthcare system
  • Finding and accessing resources for people with disabilities
